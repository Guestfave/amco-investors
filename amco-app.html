How Amco works

We make it possible to own a share of real property with a small amount of money — and we keep it honest at every step. Here's exactly how.

2 min
▶ Watch: how Amco works

A short explainer video will live here.

The idea

A good property can cost more than most people can invest alone. So we pool money from many investors — and put in our own — to buy one together. Everyone owns a share of a company that owns the property, so everyone owns a real slice of the bricks and mortar.

How your money works

We don't just buy and sit. We buy and improve, then refinance to hand most of your money back — so your cash isn't locked away for years. What stays in keeps earning.

What you get

From day one
Income immediately

The property is already let, so you earn from the moment the deal completes.

After refinance
Most of your money back

We refinance and return the bulk of your capital, usually within months.

Ongoing
A growing stake

What stays in keeps earning, plus your share of any growth when we sell.
